# ☕👨‍💻 K'fe App 

> Helping developers connect, grow together, and build meaningful communities.

## 📌 Problem

Developers often have a lot on their minds — ideas, challenges, projects, career growth, and personal development — but many struggle to find like-minded people to share and discuss them with.

Despite the growing tech community, meaningful local connections are still difficult to build.

---

## 💡 Solution

**K'fe App** is a community-driven platform that helps developers connect, collaborate, and grow together.

The platform encourages developers to:

* Meet new people in the tech community
* Share ideas and experiences
* Find small circles for café meetups
* Support and learn from one another

> “អភិវឌ្ឍន៍ខ្លួន ជួយគ្នាទៅវិញទៅមកក្នុងនាមជា community”

(Develop ourselves and help each other as a community.)

---

# ✨ Features

## ☕ Cool Café & Place Recommendations

Discover developer-friendly cafés and meetup spots:

* Good WiFi
* Quiet working environment
* Charging stations
* Great coffee & atmosphere
* Community ratings and reviews

---

## 👤 Developer Profiles

Create your developer identity:

* Tech stack & interests
* Portfolio links
* Current projects
* Availability for networking
* Social links

---

## 🤝 Café Meetup System

Easily organize or join small developer gatherings:

* Create meetup circles
* Choose café/location
* Invite nearby developers
* Schedule discussions or coding sessions

Examples:

* Startup brainstorming
* Coding together
* Career sharing
* Open-source collaboration

---

## 📢 Café Owner Promotion

Support local cafés while helping communities grow.

Café owners can:

* Promote their space
* Offer discounts for developers
* Host tech meetups
* Become community partner locations

---
